Our Story

Made with
Love & Care

"Every cucumber is chosen by hand. Every jar is packed with care. That's just how we do it."

SM Pickles started in a home kitchen in Hyderabad. The goal was simple — make pickles so good that people keep coming back for more. We use fresh vegetables, our family's original recipe, and no chemical shortcuts.

We make small batches so every jar gets the attention it deserves. When you open an SM Pickles jar, you can taste the difference.

01
We Pick Fresh

Vegetables sourced from local farms, used while fresh — never stored for weeks.

02
We Add Our Recipe

Our spice mix has been used for three generations. Simple ingredients, deep flavour.

03
We Pack by Hand

Each jar is filled, sealed, and labelled by hand. You taste the care in every bite.
